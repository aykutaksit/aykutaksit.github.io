Building at the intersection of mechanics and medicine.

I am the Co-founder and CEO of **Haystack Medical**. My career has been defined by a single, difficult challenge: the inner ear is one of the most inaccessible parts of the human body.

During my PhD at **Columbia University’s Small Scale Mechanics Laboratory**, I co-developed a proprietary fabrication method known as **Two-Photon Templated Electrodeposition (2PTE)**. This allowed us to create fully metallic, ultra-sharp microneedles capable of precision drug delivery through the Round Window Membrane.

Today, we are translating that academic breakthrough into a clinical reality. As we move toward **First-in-Human trials at Columbia NYP**, our mission is to provide the first reliable, safe access point for the next generation of inner-ear therapeutics.
