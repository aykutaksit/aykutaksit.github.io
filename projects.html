Core IP / Research 2018 – 2024

2PTE Fabrication

Two-Photon Templated Electrodeposition (2PTE) is a hybrid micro-additive manufacturing process. It combines the resolution of 3D laser writing with the structural integrity of electrodeposited metals to create sub-millimeter surgical tools.

  • Resolution down to 200nm
  • Fully Metallic (Nickel/Gold/Cobalt)
  • 20+ Peer-Reviewed Publications
